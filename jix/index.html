Features

Why players come back

Small game, obsessively crafted — every tile, sound, haptic and glow is there on purpose.

A New Puzzle Every Day

One daily Jix — four quick rounds, then the Final. Come back tomorrow and the counter ticks up again.

Compete & Share

Everyone gets the same Jix each day. Compare times with friends, then share your result and let them know how you did.

Streaks & Levels

Solve the daily puzzle to keep your streak alive and climb the levels — every player starts at White. Where you go from there is up to you.

Calm by Design

A dark glass theme for night owls and a bright light theme for daylight. Haptics and a satisfying soundscape — both yours to toggle.

How to play

Three moves to the Final

Learn it in ten seconds, chase it for years.

1

Unscramble the bank

Slide letters to move them into the right spot in the row. Reveal the hidden word. Trust your instincts. Beat the clock.

2

Read the tiles

Solid color locks a letter into the right spot. A dashed outline means the letter belongs to the Final.

3

Crack the Final

Letters you bank across all four rounds feed the last word. Solve it to finish the day — and grow your streak.
